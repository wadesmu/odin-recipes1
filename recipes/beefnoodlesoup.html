Beef Noodle Soup

This is another dish that dad makes. It is quite delicious and helps me go to sleep


Ingredients

  • 2-3 lb beef roast
  • salt and pepper
  • 2 Tablespoons oil
  • 8 cups low sodium beef browth
  • 1 envelope onion soup mix
  • 1/2 teaspoon crushed dried rosemary
  • 1/4 teaspon dried thyme
  • 3-4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 10.5 ox can cream of mushroom soup, or homemade
  • 2 large carrots, peeled and chopped
  • 2 ribs celery, chopped
  • 24 oz good quality egg noodles
  • Salt and pepper, for taste
  • Sauteed mushrooms

Courtesy of foodnetwork.com

Directions

  1. Trim any fat pieces from the roast and cut into 1 inch cubes.
  2. Add oil to a large pot or dutch oven over high heat
  3. Season beef on all sides with salt and pepper
  4. Once oil is hot, add beef to the pot and snear on all sides
  5. Stir in beef broth, onion soup mix, rosemary, thyme, and garlic cloves. Bring mixture to a boil, then reduce heat to low, cover and cook for 1 hour.
  6. Add mushroom soup, celery and carrots and cook for another 20 minutes. Add egg noodles and cook for 5-10 minutes until tender. Stir in saut√©ed mushrooms before serving, if desired.
  7. Leftover soup will keep in the refrigerator for 3-4 days.
